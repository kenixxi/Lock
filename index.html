Stay locked in your best.

Lock exists because champ select can get messy fast. Players often hover random picks, overthink matchups, or forget the champions they actually trained. Lock keeps your own champion pool visible and warns you when you drift off it.

It is built to be simple: choose your 2–5 best champions, sync your public profile, and let Lock quietly help you stay disciplined.

Why Lock exists

Lock is not made to play the game for you. It is made to protect the work you already put in. If you have a ranked champion pool, Lock keeps that pool close, visible, and easy to respect.

Less overload

League has too many choices in champ select. Lock narrows your attention back to your own plan.

Better discipline

When you hover something outside your pool, Lock gives a small reminder before you lock in.

Simple profiles

Show your public nickname, rank, stats, and champion pool without exposing your email.

What Lock does

Champion pool

Choose 2–5 champions as your personal ranked pool.

Champ-select alert

Lock watches local champ select and warns when your current hover/pick is outside your pool.

Stats and search

Sync rank, winrate, KDA, champion pool, and search public Lock profiles.
