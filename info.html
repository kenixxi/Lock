About Lock

Lock is a desktop app created to help League of Legends players stay disciplined with their own champion pool. It focuses on one simple idea: play what you practiced, avoid random panic picks, and stay locked in your best.

What Lock is

Lock is a lightweight desktop companion for champion select. You choose a personal champion pool, and Lock helps you notice when you move outside of it.

Why it exists

Ranked games are easier to improve in when you have discipline. Lock exists to reduce overthinking, limit champion select chaos, and keep your attention on champions you trust.

Who made it

Lock was created and owned by KeniX. The app, website, and idea are built around making ranked champion pools easier to follow.

Main features

Lock is designed to stay clean, simple, and useful. It does not try to overload you with information. It focuses only on your own profile, your own pool, and your own discipline.

Champion pool

Pick 2–5 champions as your personal Lock pool. This pool becomes your main ranked identity inside the app and on your public profile.

Champ select detection

Lock checks the local League Client while champion select is open. If your current hover or pick is outside your selected pool, the app shows a small reminder.

Public profile

Your public profile can show your nickname, summoner name, rank, winrate, KDA, and champion pool. Your email is not shown publicly.

User search

Search other Lock users and view their public champion pools and profile information. This makes the app feel more connected while staying simple.

Auto-sync

While Lock is open, it can automatically refresh Solo/Duo current split stats every 30 minutes using the local League Client connection.

Clean desktop design

Lock uses a dark, modern interface with a focus on readability, calm visuals, and a clean app experience.

Privacy and safety

Email privacy

Your email is used only for account login. Public profiles are based on nicknames, not email addresses.

Local League connection

Lock reads local League Client information from your own PC while the app is open. This is used for champion select detection and stat syncing.

No gameplay automation

Lock does not play the game for you, pick champions for you, or automate gameplay. It is a focus and discipline tool.
